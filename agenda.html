Due to risks associated with global travel and the spread COVID-19, the Maps for the Web workshop in 2020 will be online only, using a distributed, asynchronous model to encourage in-depth participation regardless of time zones.

This is a new format for a W3C or OGC Workshop, and we're still finalizing the details of what it will look like. Suggestions from registered participants are welcome.

A detailed workshop agenda will be established based on received submissions. The current plan is for themed video sessions (panels or presentations) posted daily for roughly two weeks (21 September − 2 October 2020). Questions and open discussion will then continue using online forums, which will remain open for a month afterwards.

In parallel, we will host breakout and hack sessions, using a mix of video presentations, live video chat or IRC chat, and written forums.

To suggest talk, panel, or breakout session, or a hack project, please see the call for participation.
